Living in An Thuong

An Thuong sits just behind My Khe Beach in Da Nang and has become the city's most popular expat enclave. Narrow lanes are packed with coffee shops, co-working spaces, yoga studios, and restaurants serving food from around the world.

Most apartments here are walk-up studios and one-bedrooms within a 10-minute stroll of the sand. Rents are higher than the city average, but residents trade that for a relaxed, walkable lifestyle close to the ocean.
